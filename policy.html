Amanat is designed to help users post, search, and manage listings for lost or found items. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that any personal information you provide is handled securely. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

We may collect information such as your name, contact details, and item descriptions when you create or respond to listings. This information is stored securely and used solely to facilitate communication between users. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to help connect users.

Permissions Required
